# Dimensions ## Generative art and augmented reality On August 2019, I made an augmented reality generative art experience for an exhibit featuring works of Canva employees during the local office's 5th anniversary celebration. I joined because it sounded like a fun opportunity to push myself and learn new things! I knew right away that the art medium had to be **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JS**, and I wanted it to somehow be an interactive experience. After struggling for inspiration (and after one failed attempt), I’ve come up with a **generative art** and **augmented reality** concept. The general plan was to make two components — one physical print and one AR app. I wrote about it in detail in the following posts: * [Part one: Generative art](part-one-generative-art/) * [Part two: Augmented reality](part-two-augmented-reality/)
