Polies

Ludum Dare 24 entry (Theme: Evolution)
The pieces evolve using genetic algorithm.

I became really interested in biologically-inspired computing, like genetic algorithms (a simulation of evolution), artificial neural networks (a simulation of the brain), artificial life (a simulation of ... guess what.). Here, the pieces (polyminoes) breed with each other to produce different kinds of pieces, evolving over generations.
